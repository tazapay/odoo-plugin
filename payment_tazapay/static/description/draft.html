Tazapay Payment Acquirer

Tazapay Payment Acquirer is a very useful Odoo module which allows to receive payment using Tazapay.

Features

Commission Access Rights.

Commission manager and user acceess rights, only manager can create and configure commission, and user can see commission line, and analysis.

Sales Commission on Payment.

While payment is done by customer commission will be calculated based on sales person.

Sales Commission on Invoice.

While validating invoice commission will be calculated based on sale person.

Sales Commission based on Partner, Margin, Product & Product Category.

You are allowed to give commission based on Partner, Margin, Product and product category.

Quick Demo

Select a Product

Customers visits your website and selects a product and adds to cart.


Checkout

When the customer has added all items to cart, the next is checkout.


Tazapay Acquirer Selection

The customer chooses to pay with tazapay and a redirection to tazapay happens. At this point an escrow transaction will be created and user will need to complete transaction by paying on tazapay website.
Note: If the customer has an account, then he probably has an address already, but if not, the customer will be asked to fill in the billing and shipping address.


Tazapay Payment Option

At tazapay your customers can select how they wish to pay, and these processes are synchronous and asynchronous.


Tazapay Card Payment Option

Once the user selects card payment option. The credit card information will be asked and user can proceed to pay. (the card information used is a test card)


Tazapay Transaction Feedback

The customer gets a feedback of the transaction, if it was successful or if it failed, then a redirection to the merchant's website happens


Merchant's Transaction Feedback

No matter the feedback from tazapay, the buyer will get a feedback on the merchant's website also. In this case, we had a successful payment and the merchant's website also showed a confirmed and successful order
