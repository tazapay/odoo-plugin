Checkout with Tazapay

Tazapay is a very useful Odoo module that allows your webstore to provide multiple localised payment methods for your buyers during checkout.

Features

  • Offer multiple international payment methods on your checkout page from buyers in over 173 countries.

  • Enable customers to pay with local methods in over 70 major markets.

  • Low-cost secured payments for buyers and sellers at best in class FX rates.

  • Wide variety of payment methods accepted: Bank Redirect, QR Codes, Local Bank Transfers, eWallets, Mastercard, VISA, Voucher payments and more.

  • Fully compliant with local and international regulations, all relevant trade documents are provided. .

How do payments via Tazapay work?

  • After installing Tazapay's app, you will be given options to enable or disable payment options for your buyers. Payment methods will vary by country depending on where your buyer is located.

  • When your buyer checks out with Tazapay, they will be redirected to Tazapay's checkout page, where we will show multiple payment options based on your buyer's country.

  • The buyer makes their payment as instructed in their chosen method, and then they will receive a notification if the payment is successful.

  • As a seller, you can choose to receive your payment either in your own local currency or in USD, which will be directly deposited into your local bank account.
